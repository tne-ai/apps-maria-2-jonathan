Over these years, the total GRP has shown a consistent upward trajectory. In 2019, the GRP was approximately $244.28 billion. Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, 2020 saw a slight increase to $244.82 billion. The following year, 2021, marked a significant rise to $268.87 billion, indicating a robust economic recovery. This upward trend continued in 2022, with the GRP reaching $296.68 billion, and further increased to $308.71 billion in 2023, reflecting sustained economic growth. Alongside the total GRP, the per capita GRP also demonstrated growth, starting at $73,347 in 2019 and increasing to $74,278 in 2020. By 2021, it rose notably to $82,100, aligning with the overall GRP growth.
