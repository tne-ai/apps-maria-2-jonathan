To analyze the Gross Regional Product (GRP) of San Diego County between 2019 and 2023, we will examine the data provided in the `sec1-2_San Diego County_per_capita_grp_data.csv` file. ### San Diego County GRP Analysis (2019-2023)

1. **2019:**
   - **Total GRP:** $244,278,846,457.50
   - **Population:** 3,330,458

2. **2020:**

3. **2021:**

4. **2022:**

   - **Population:** 3,276,208
   - **GRP per Capita:** $90,557.10

5. **2023:**
   - **Total GRP:** $308,710,843,090.86
   - **Population:** 3,252,468
   - **GRP per Capita:** $94,915.87
   - **Analysis:** The GRP reached its highest level in this period, with a further increase in GRP per capita, reflecting continued economic expansion.

### Summary:
- Over the five-year period, San Diego County experienced consistent growth in its Gross Regional Product, with the total GRP increasing from approximately $244.3 billion in 2019 to $308.7 billion in 2023.
- The GRP per capita also showed a steady increase, rising from $73,346.92 in 2019 to $94,915.87 in 2023. This indicates that the economic output per person in the county improved significantly.
- The population of San Diego County showed a slight decline from 2019 to 2023, which, combined with the rising GRP, contributed to the increase in GRP per capita.

Overall, San Diego County demonstrated strong economic growth between 2019 and 2023, with both total GRP and GRP per capita showing positive trends.
